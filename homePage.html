Services

        GUIDR is the future of the adventure vacation world. We strive to provide both recreational and professional adventurers a way to primarily track their past trips. Once they have these trips tracked, they will be able to market them to prospective adventure goers. We will give people the option to publicly display any of their adventures. If they would rather keep them private for personal use they will be able to do that as well.
        We also want people to make accounts so they can save any potential trips they want to go on. They should be able to share with other people the trips that interest them. We are passionate about the outdoors, and we hope that we can help provide that same fulfillment to everyone else. Looking forward to seeing everyone out on the trails!
